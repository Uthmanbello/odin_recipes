Rice

Description

Rice is the most common food in Africa.

It is the most widely consumed. It is easy to prepare and delicious to taste.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Seasoning

Steps

  1. Boil the rice in hot water for about 45 mins.
  2. Add salt to it to taste.
  3. Add the seasoning.
  4. Let it boil for another 5 mins
  5. The rice is ready to serve.
