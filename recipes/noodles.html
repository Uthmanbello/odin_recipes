Noodles

Description

The biggest advantage of noodles is that it is very cheap and easy to prepare.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Water
  • Pepper
  • Seasoning
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles for about 5 mins in 1 cup of hot water.
  2. Add the pepper and seasoning to it.
  3. Add about 2 table spoons of oil.
  4. Let it boil for another 5 mins
  5. The noodles is ready to serve.
